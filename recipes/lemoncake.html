Lemon Cake

Sweet, zesty godliness!

Who doesn't love lemon cake? I love lemon cake. You're here so you must love lemon cake. If you can find a person that doesn't love lemon cake I will personally shove some so far up their keister they'll be tasting it til they die and they'll have a good reason to hate it.

We don't really need to go that far though do we? We can just ignore all the lemon cake haters and live our own perfect lemon cake loving life! And what better way to do that than making some freaking lemon cake?? Let's get to it toots...

Ingredients

  • Lemon
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients together
  2. Pour them into a cake pan
  3. Throw it the oven for a while. When it smells like a summer pool party gone right pull that bad boy out.

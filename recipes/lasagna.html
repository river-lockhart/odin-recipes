Lasagna

Cheesy, beefy goodness!

Who doesn't love lasagna? I love lasagna. You're here so you must love lasagna. If you can find a person that doesn't love lasagna I will personally shove some so far up their keister they'll be tasting it til they die and they'll have a good reason to hate it.

We don't really need to go that far though do we? We can just ignore all the lasagna haters and live our own perfect lasagna loving life! And what better way to do that than making some freaking lasagna?? Let's get to it toots...

Ingredients

  • Some lasagna noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Beef
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Cook the beef with seasonings and noodles
  2. Throw em in a lasagna tray together
  3. Baste it with the tomatos life force
  4. Cover it in cheese and I mean COVER it
  5. Throw it the oven for a while. When it smells like a Sopranos house party pull that bad boy out.
